@moku-labs/room — sandbox

Pick a role. Open Stage on the shared screen (TV/laptop), then open Controller on each phone (or scan the QR the stage shows).

📺 Stageshared screen — hosts the room 🎮 Controllerphone — joins by code/QR

Default backbone is real WebRTC (publicRendezvous, Nostr). Append ?signaling=memory for the single-context in-memory bus, or ?backbone=torrent for the BitTorrent fallback.
